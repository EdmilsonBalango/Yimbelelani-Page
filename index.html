Worship in Xitswa.
Read and sing hymns anywhere.

Yimbelelani is a digital hymnal designed to preserve and promote Christian hymns in Xitswa, a local language of Southern Mozambique. It includes translations of well-known hymns from Portuguese, presented in a clean and readable format.

Xitswa Hymnal Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-29

Xitswa Hymnal is a hymnal app focused on providing Christian hymns in Xitswa and translations of well-known Portuguese hymns. We value your privacy and designed the app to minimize data collection.

Data we collect

The app is intended to work without requiring internet access for core usage. If future versions introduce optional online features, this policy will be updated accordingly.
